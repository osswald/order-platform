Tenant settings apply hire-company-wide for your hire company. Here you maintain company data and default receipt templates.

## Who has access?

- **Hire company admin** — full access
- **Platform admin** — after selecting the active hire company in the sidebar

Organisation admins and members do not see this area. See **Roles and access**.

## Master data

Under **Tenant settings** edit:

- Hire company **name** (required)
- **Address**, postal code, city, country

These details may appear on receipts and in communication with customer organisations.

## Receipt templates

In **Receipt printing** configure templates at hire company level:

- Default profiles for receipt and payment receipts
- Print options that serve as templates for organisations and events

Organisations and individual events can override with their own receipt profiles — hire company templates are the starting point.

## Distinction from organisations

| Level | Location | Purpose |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Hire company | Tenant settings | Tenant master data, global receipt templates |
| Organisation | Organisations | Customer master data, accounting, Stripe |
| Event | Event configuration → Receipts | Event-specific receipt profiles |
