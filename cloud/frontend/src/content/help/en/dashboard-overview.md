The dashboard is your home page after login. It summarises the current state of your organisation.

## What you see

- **Attention** — hints about open tasks or configuration gaps
- **Current events** — events in preparation or live
- **Recent activity** — recently changed records in your organisation

## Switch organisation

Select the desired organisation under **Active organisation** in the sidebar. All org-specific areas (events, articles, waiters, etc.) refer to this selection.

Platform administrators also choose an **Active hire company** before operational data is loaded.
