Under **Appliance lendings**, members of your organisation see which appliances are currently or soon to be lent out.

## What is shown

- **Currently lent appliances** — active lendings with period and appliance type
- **Planned lendings** — future reservations

The view is **read-only**. Lendings are planned and assigned by the hire company in administration.

## Choose organisation

The table refers to the **Active organisation** selected in the sidebar. Without an organisation a hint to select one is shown.

## Appliance types

Typical entries: server (Raspberry Pi), printer, mobile waiter devices, and other installations. The type helps with assignment on site.

## Questions about lending

Contact your hire company or organisation administrator if appliances are missing, periods need adjusting, or a device must be replaced.
